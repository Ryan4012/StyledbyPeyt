About

Hi! I'm Peyton, a cosmetologist and hair lover with a passion for blonding, color, and all things beautiful. I've spent the last few years growing in this industry, perfecting my craft, and learning how to bring out the natural, glam, and elegant style that helps each of my clients feel like the best version of themselves. I'm super friendly and love connecting with the people who sit in my chair. My goal is to create a relaxing, comfortable space where you feel heard, pampered, and truly beautiful in your own skin. Great hair starts with trust—and I'm here for you every step of the way.
