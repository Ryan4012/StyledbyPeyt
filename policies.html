Salon Policies — Respecting Your Time & Mine

Thank you for choosing Styled by Peyt! To provide all clients with the best experience possible, please review the following policies regarding appointments, cancellations, and rescheduling.


Booking & Deposit Policy

A non-refundable deposit of $25 to $50 is required to secure your appointment. This deposit will be applied toward your service total at checkout.

Cancellation Policy

I kindly ask for at least 24 hours notice if you need to cancel your appointment.

Cancellations made less than 24 hours before the scheduled time will result in a loss of your initial deposit.

Repeated late cancellations may require full prepayment for future bookings.

No-Show Policy

If you do not show up for your appointment without notice, you lose your inital deposit. Future appointments may require full prepayment.

Rescheduling

You're welcome to reschedule your appointment up to 24 hours in advance with no penalty. If less notice is given, it will be treated as a late cancellation.

Late Arrivals

If you arrive more than 15 minutes late, I may need to adjust or reschedule your service to stay on time for other clients. Late arrivals may be considered a no-show at my discretion.


These policies ensure fairness to all clients and help me manage time effectively. Thank you for your understanding and for supporting my small business! — Peyt
